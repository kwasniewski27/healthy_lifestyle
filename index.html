NUTRITION

Evidence shows the health benefits of a diet high in whole grains, vegetables, fruit, legumes and nuts, and low in salt, free sugars and fats, particularly saturated and trans fats.

EXERCISE

Popular ways to be active include walking, cycling, wheeling, sports, active recreation and play, and can be done at any level of skill and for enjoyment by everybody.

SLEEP

  • Get sick less often
  • Stay at a healthy weight
  • Improve your heart health and metabolism
  • Lessen the risk of motor vehicle crashes and related injury or death
  • Improve your attention and memory to better perform daily activities

Diet and Exercise Plan

Diet and Exercise Advice

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day by drinking plenty of water.
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars for better health.
  • Include fruits and vegetables in every meal for essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Exercise regularly: a mix of cardio and strength training is ideal.
  • Get enough sleep each night to support recovery and muscle growth.
  • Warm-up and cool down properly to prevent injuries during exercise.
  • Track your progress to stay motivated and adjust your plan as needed.
